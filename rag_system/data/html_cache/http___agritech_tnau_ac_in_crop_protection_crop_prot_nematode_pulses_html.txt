Pigeonpea cyst nematode :
Heterodera cajani
Symptoms
Infected plants show yellowing, stunting, poor vigour and pod formation. Several crops like pigeonpea, cowpea, mungbean, soybean, black gram and sesame are seriously damaged by the nematode.
At seedling stage of plant, pearl-like or lemon-shaped white female can be found attached with roots.
Management
Summer ploughing of fields during hot months.
Crop rotation with non host crops for 2-3 years.
Soil application
Pseudomonas fluorescens
or
Trichoderma viride
@ 2.5 kg/ha at the time of sowing.
Seed treatment with
Pseudomonas fluorescens
and
Trichoderma viride
@ 5+5 g/kg seed.
