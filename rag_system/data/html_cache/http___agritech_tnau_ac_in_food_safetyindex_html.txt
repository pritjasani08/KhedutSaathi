Important Food Safety Logos
FSSAI Logo & License No. is required on the pre-packaged food product.
Green mark logo on the label of the pre-packaged Vegetarian food product.
Brown mark logo on the label of the pre-packaged Non-Vegetarian food product.
Yellow mark logo on the label of the pre-packaged food that contaings egg.
AGMARK is a certification mark employed on agricultural products in India.
Logo indicates that the food is certified as being an organic product.
ISI Mark logo should be available on the Packaged drinking water.
Food Irradiation logo indicates that the food has been processed using radiation technique.
