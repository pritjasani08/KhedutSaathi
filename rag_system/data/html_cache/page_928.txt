Hill Area Development Program
Related Link
OBJECTIVE
To  Improve the Socio Economic Status of the Nilgiri’s Farmers by giving subsidy to  the farmers.
To  increase the area under Horticultural Crops
SCHEME  COMPONENT & SUBSIDY PATTERN
Distribution  of Oil Engine with 25% Subsidy
Distribution  of Hand Sprayers with 25% Subsidy
Distribution  of Power Tiller with 25% Subsidy
Distribution  of Agricultural Implements with 25% Subsidy
Distribution  of Tea clones with 50% Subsidy
SELECTION  OF BENEFICIARIES
Small  and Marginal Farmers are eligible
IMPLEMENTATION
The  Scheme is implemented only in Nilgiri District
During  In 2012 – 13, it has been to purposed implement with a financial outlay  of
Rs.  240.00 Lakhs to cover an area of 1850 Ha.
Last Update October 2014
