Stress Management
:: Salinity
Mitigation of
Salt Stress
Seed hardening with NaCl (10 mM concentration)
Application of gypsum @ 50% Gypsum Requirement (GR)
Incorporation of daincha (6.25 t/ha) in soil before
planting
Foliar spray of 0.5 ppm brassinolode for increasing
photosynthetic activity
Foliar spray of 2% DAP + 1% KCl (MOP) during critical
stages
Spray of 100 ppm salicylic acid
Spray of 40 ppm of NAA for arresting pre-mature fall of
flowers / buds / fruits
Extra dose of nitrogen (25%) in excess of the
recommended
Split application of N and K fertilizers
Seed treatment + soil application + foliar spray of
Pink Pigmented Facultative Methnaotrops (PPFM) @ 106 as a source of
cytokinins
