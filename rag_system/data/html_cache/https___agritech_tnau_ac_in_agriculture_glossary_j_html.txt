Glossary :: J
June
budding
A
season of budding exists between late may and early June in the northern
hemisphere and November to December in the southern hemisphere.
June
drop
The final post-bloom
shedding of fruits, often occur in late May or June.
Juvenile
phase
The
phase of vegetative growth of a plant before which it cannot be induced to
produce flowers. This period may vary from plant to plant, e.g., rice plants
have juvenile phase of one month.
