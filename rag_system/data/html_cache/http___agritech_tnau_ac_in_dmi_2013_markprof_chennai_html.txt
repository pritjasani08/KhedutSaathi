Chennai Koyambedu Market Profile
Market Name
Chennai - Koyambedu Market
Market Type
Retail market
State
Tamil Nadu
Market Infrastructure Facilities
The wholesalers have their own shop to store their commodity
Type of Auction
No auction. According to the demand and supply of the commodities, the whole sellers will fix up the rates.
Market Commission Percentage
4% to 10% for all commodities
Type of packs Preferred
Drumstick, Ladiesfinger (bhendi), Cluster beans, Mango and Chilli
: Gunny bags
Carrot, Radish, Knol-khol, Beetroot, Capsicum and Baby corn
: Polythene bags
Tomato
: Wooden box or plastic box
Mode of Payment
Cash
Buyers Profile
Retailers, vendors and consumers
Market Working Hours /Holidays
2.00 am to 12.00 am / No holiday
Toll Charges
Rs.50.00 to Rs.100.00
Rs.5.00 - Rs.15.00/bag according to distance from the shop and the entrance
Market loading Charges
Rs.5.00 - Rs.15.00 (per bag) according to distance from the shop and the entrance.
Market Unloading Charges
Rs.5.00/bag
Logistic Details
Regular services for all destinations
