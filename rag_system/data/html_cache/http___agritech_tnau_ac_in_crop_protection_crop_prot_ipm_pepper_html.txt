Crop protection :: IPM :: Pepper
I
ntegrated pest management of pepper
Cultural
Hoe the soil under the vines in Sept – Oct and Dec – Jan to destroy the pupae of pollu beetle in soil
Use of resistant varieties line Uthirankotta 1 and II, TMB V and Shimoga against pollu beetle
Chemical
Spray of dimethoate or quinalphos 0.1% in late June and early Oct.
Top shoot borer can be controlled by the spray of dimethoate or phosphomidon 0.05%.
Spray with malathion 0.1% or dimethoate 0.05% to keep gall forming thrips population under check
Biological
Field release of parasites like
Apanteles
sp,
Eduderus
and
Goniozus
are effective in reducing the top shoot borer incidence
