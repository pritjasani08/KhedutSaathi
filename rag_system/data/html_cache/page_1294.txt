Announcement
Director’s Message
As the Director of the Central Institute for Cotton Research (CICR), Nagpur, I am committed to advancing cutting-edge research and sustainable practices in cotton cultivation. Our institute is dedicated to developing high-quality cotton varieties and supporting farmers through innovative solutions. We focus on enhancing productivity, promoting environmental sustainability, and fostering collaboration with stakeholders. Together, we aim to shape a brighter future for cotton farmers through innovative research and development.
Dr. V.N. Waghmare
Director, CICR Nagpur
