INTEGRATED HORTICULTURE DEVELOPMENT SCHEME
This scheme is being
implemented in all the 29 districts of Tamil Nadu excluding Chennai. Under this
scheme elite planting materials, high yielding/hybrid vegetable seeds are being
distributed to horticultural crops cultivating farmers at 50% subsidized cost.
During 2007-08, this scheme implanted with an outlay of Rs.266.610 lakhs. The
scheme is proposed to be implemented with an outlay of Rs.338 lakhs during
2008-09.
Tomato, Chilli and Bhendi
hybrid seeds and also the seedlings of Mango, Guava and Sapota are distributed
through IHDS scheme.
