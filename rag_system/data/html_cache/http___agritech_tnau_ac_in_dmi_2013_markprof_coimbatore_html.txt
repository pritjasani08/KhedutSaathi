Coimbatore Market Profile
Market Name
Coimbatore Market
Market Type
Wholesale Market
State
Tamil Nadu
Market Infrastructure Facilities
55 to 60 shops, approach road, shelter, communication, transport and labour facilities
Type of Auction
Open auction for all vegetables take place in 5 shops. Auction time: From 7.00 am to 10.00 am
Market Commission Percentage
Commission varies from 2% to 10% for all vegetable
Type of packs Preferred
Tomato
:Wooden boxes.
Potato, roots and tubers
:Gunny bags.
Drumstick, Ladiesfinger (bhendi), Cluster beans, Mango and Chilli
: Gunny bags
Mode of Payment
Cash
Buyers Profile
Retailers and Vendors
Market Working Hours /Holidays
No holiday
Toll Charges
Two Wheeler: Rs.5.00 / vehicle
Auto : Rs. 35.00 / vehicle
Four Wheeler: Rs.35.00 / vehicle
Lorry - Eicher : Rs.110.00 / vehicle | Tipper : Rs. 200
Market loading Charges
Rs.3.00 - 5.00 / Small bags
Rs.10.00 / Big bags
Rs.2.50 - 4.00 / Tomato tray.
Market Unloading Charges
Rs.3.50 - 5.00 / Small bags
Rs.10.00 / Big bags
Rs.2.50 - 4.00 / Tomato tray.
Logistic Details
Trucks, mini trucks, mini vans and three wheelers are available in plenty; some buyers use their own vehicle
