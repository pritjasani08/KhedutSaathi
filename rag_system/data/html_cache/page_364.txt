TNAU PULSE WONDER
A booster with nutrients and growth regulators for Pulses

Decreases flower  shedding
Increases yield up  to 20 per cent
Increases drought  tolerance

Dose
:
2 kg / acre
Spray volume
:
200 litres
Stage of spray

Peak flowering
Add adequate  quantity of wetting agent
