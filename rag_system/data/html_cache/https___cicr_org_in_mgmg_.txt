Mera Gaon Mera Gaurav
Mera Gaon Mera Gaurav (MGMG) program has been implemented by ICAR- CICR Nagpur and its Regional Stations at Coimbatore and Sirsa as per the guidelines during 2024. The program was implemented in 55 adopted villages in 11 clusters by 45 Scientists and 7 Teams. The details of Scientists involved and villages adopted are given below.
