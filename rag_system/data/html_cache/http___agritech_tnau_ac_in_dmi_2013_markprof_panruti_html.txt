Panruti Market Profile
Market Name
Panruti Market
Market Type
Whole sale and Retail Market (Rathinampillai Daily Vegetable Market)
State
Tamil Nadu
Market Infrastructure Facilities
Nearly 150 shops with an area around 3 acres.
Type of Auction
No Auction. The whole sellers fix up the rates according to the demand and supply of the commodities.
Market Commission Percentage
Commission varies from 3% to 8% for all fruits and vegetables.
Type of packs Preferred
Drumstick
,
Bhendi
,
Mango,
Chilli
,
Carrot
,
Radish
,
Knol-Khol
,
Beetroot
,
Capsicum
,
Baby corn
: Gunny Bags
Tomato
: Plastic box
Mode of Payment
Cash
Buyers Profile
Retailers , vendors and consumers
Market Working Hours /Holidays
4.00 am to 10.00 am
No holiday
Toll Charges
No charges for two, three and four wheelers
Market loading Charges
Rs.5.00 to Rs.10.00 (according to the distance from the shop and the entrance)
Market Unloading Charges
Rs.5.00 to Rs.10.00 (according to the distance from the shop and the entrance)
Logistic Details
Regular service for all destinations, some buyers use their own vehicle.
