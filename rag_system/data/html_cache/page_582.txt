Glossary :: X
Xanthophyll
:
A yellow or orange  carotid pigment associated with chlorophyll in chloroplasts, also present in  certain chromoplasts.
Xylem
:
The principal water-conducting  non-living tissue in vascular plants characterized by the presence of tracheids;  may also contain vessels, parenchyma cells, fibers, and sclerides.
