Thirami-Treated Cow Urine (TTCU)
Ingredients:
(a) 5 liters cow urine, (b) 250 gms jiggery, (c) 250 ml ET solution.
Preparation:
Mix all and let ferment for 7-10 days.
Usage:
Use within 30 days.  For spraying:  Mix 3-5 ml in one liter water.  For irrigation, use 20-30 liters per acre.This controls pests and diseases.
