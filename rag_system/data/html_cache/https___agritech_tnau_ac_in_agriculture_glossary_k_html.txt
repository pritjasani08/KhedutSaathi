Krishi
Vigyan Kendras (KVK)
The schemes on KVK were
launched by the ICAR, Govt. of India, during 1976 as an innovative institution
for vocational training in agriculture and allied area. Skill training is
imparted to the trainees through the methods of teaching by doing and learning
by doing. The KVK aims at practical training to practicing farmers, farm women
as well as school drop-outs who acquire training for self-employment.
