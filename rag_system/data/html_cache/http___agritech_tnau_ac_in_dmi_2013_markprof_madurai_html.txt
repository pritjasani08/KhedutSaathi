Madurai Market Profile
Market Name
Madurai Market
Market Type
Whole sale and Retail
market
(Central Market Vegetables and Perishable Commodities Merchants Coordinated
Association (Mattuthavani) - Wholesale & Retail Madurai - Town Central
Market (Retail))
State
Tamil Nadu
Market Infrastructure Facilities
Nearly 200 registered
shops. Well facilitated with approach road, transport, communication and labour
facilities.
Type of Auction
Vegetables
: Fixed price. The price varies according to the current situation /
demand of the product.
Fruits
: Open auction for Mango,Lemon,Jack fruit,Guava,Amla.
Auction time
: 9.00 pm.
Market Commission Percentage
Commission varies from 5%
to 10% for all fruits and vegetables.
Type of packs Preferred
Tomato
: Plastic boxes,
Potato
,
Onion
,
Cabbage
,
Cauliflower
,
Mango
,
Chilli
,
Drumstick
,
Bhendi
:
Gunny bags,
Other vegetables
: Polythene bags,
Apple, Grapes
: Plastic and Card-board boxes,
Pineapple
: Gunny bags,
Sathgudi
: Polythene bags,
Other fruits
: Plastic boxes.
Mode of Payment
Cash.
Buyers Profile
Wholesale agents, Retailers
and small vendors from local and around the village of Madurai district.
Market Working Hours /Holidays
Wholesale: 3.00 am  to 11:00 pm
Retail: 3:00 am to 2:00 pm
monthly Last Sunday Holiday.
Toll Charges
Two wheeler: Rs.3.00/ Vehicle
Tricycle: Rs.5.00/ Vehicle
Car:Rs.10/ Vehicle
Minidor Van: Rs.25.00/ Vehicle
Lorry: Rs.30.00/ Vehicle
Market loading Charges
Rs.3.00-5.00/Per Box
(according to distance from the shop and entrance).
Market Unloading Charges
Rs.3.00/Per Box
Total Loading and unloading
Charges
Rs.40.00/(50 Box)
Logistic Details
Tricycles, Tempo, Auto,Lorry
(Minimum 3-20 tonnes capacity).
