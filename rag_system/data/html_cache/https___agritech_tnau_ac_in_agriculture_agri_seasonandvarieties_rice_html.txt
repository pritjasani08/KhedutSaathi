Note of Caution of the varieties:
IR
50 and ADT43 are recommended for Kar, Sornavari and Kuruvai seasons and
should not be grown during cold weather period. Improved White Ponni is also
susceptible to blast and care should be taken on plant protection measures. All
samba/late samba season varieties are likely to get infected with false smut
and hence prophylactic spraying has to be adopted.
