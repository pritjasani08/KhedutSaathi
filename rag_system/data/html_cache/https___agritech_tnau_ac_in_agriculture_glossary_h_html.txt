Glossary :: H
Herbicide
(i) A chemical used for killing or reduces the negative
effects of weeds.
Hill
placement
Method
of applying fertilizer either in bands or near the plants. This method is
usually employed when relatively small quantities are to be applied in pinches
at the base of each plant of cotton, maize, cauliflower, cabbage, brinjal,
tomato and other vegetable crops by this method.
Heifer
A young female bovine.
Homogenization
The
process of physically reducing the particle size of fat in milk, thus enabling
even distribution of fat throughout the milk.
Hortipasture
Fruit trees are raised
on the contours while the area between the trees is converted into a pasture for
animals.
Hydroponic
Growing plants in
aqueous solution of essential nutrients.
