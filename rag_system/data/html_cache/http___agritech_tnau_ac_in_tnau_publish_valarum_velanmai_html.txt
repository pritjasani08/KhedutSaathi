TNAU Agritech Portal :: Uzhavarin Valarum Velanmai
Uzhavarin Valarum Velanmai:
The Tamil
monthly magazine of Tamil Nadu Agricultural
University is being published from the
Directorate of Extension Education, TNAU, Coimbatore
– 3 for the benefit of the farming community since 1975. From the month of
January-2008 onwards, the Uzhavarin Valarum Velanmai magazine has been face lifted in its
quality with printing of Multi Colour Insert that attracted more number of
subscribers.
During the year 2007-08, eight
special issues of Uzhavarin Valarum Velanmai viz., April-2007 as Education Special,
May-2007 as Horticulture Special, July-2007 as Organic Farming Special,
September-2007 as Agricultural Rural Management Special, October-2007 as Plant
Protection Special, January-2008 as Seed Technology Special, February-2008 as
Environmental Science Special and March-2008 as Bamboo Special  were prepared . As on October 2008, there are
5084 anuual subscribers and 5616 life subscribers.
