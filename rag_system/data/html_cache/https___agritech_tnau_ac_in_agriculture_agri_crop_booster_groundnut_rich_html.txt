TNAU GROUNDNUT RICH
A booster with nutrients and growth regulators for Groundnut

More flower
retention
Improves pod filling
Increases pod
yield up to 15 per cent
Improves drought tolerance

Dose
:
2 kg / acre
Spray volume
:
200 litres
Stage of spray
:
Peak flowering and pod
development
Add adequate quantity of wetting agent
