TEA  PRODUCER SOCIETY
Primary  objective of QUP was to achieve Consistency in Quality in small tea sector
.
To achieve this, it was proposed to  work on the concept of setting up of Tea Producers’ Society (TPS) with the  large number of small growers who are loyal members supplying leaf to the 20  Quality tea procurement and service centres (QTPSC) under QUP which would  attract more number of Growers into the Quality fold.  The Tea Board to this effect also launched  special scheme for those setting up Tea Producers’ Society the details of which  are give below;
Each society should  have at least 20 small grower members under its command and it should have been  registered as per the provisions of the Societies Act.
Each society and its  members (self help group) should be registered with Tea Board.
The major activities of  the Society should include:
Extension – Technology  and information dissemination
Leaf collection,  storage and transportation
Procurement and supply  of inputs such as fertilizers, plant protection chemicals, sprayers, pruning  machines etc., to the members of the society.
I
n  order to encourage the small growers form Tea Producers’ Society (TPS) Tea  Board under the 10th plan period has given special subsidies for  setting up of TPS as detailed below;
Rate of  subsidy for the Tea producers societies (Self Help Groups):
50% subsidy for  purchase of vehicle for transporting green leaves from fields to factory.
100% subsidy for  setting up of leaf collection sheds with a ceiling limit of Rs.30,000/-
Actual cost will be  reimbursed for purchase of Plastic crates, leaf carry bags and weighing  machines
25%  subsidy for purchase of Pruning Machines subject to ceiling limit of Rs.5,000/-  per machine for one Self Help Groups
Hence  during the period it was proposed to convert all the 20 Quality Tea Procurement  and Service centres into Tea Producers’ Societies and Awareness campaigns were  conducted to motivate the SHGs to form TPS.   The details of the society formed in different parts of Nilgiris after  QUP are as below;
