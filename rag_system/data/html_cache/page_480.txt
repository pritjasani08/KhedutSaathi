Season and Varieties  :: Cotton  :: HDPS
COTTON
HDPS with Complete Mechanization in Cotton
Season
Summer irrigated (Feb-Mar)
Winter irrigated(Aug  Sep)
Winter rainfed (SepAug)
Rice fallow (Feb-Mar)
Variety
CO17  compact variety, 135 days duration, single harvest
Field preparation with application of FYM and biofertilizers
Prepare the field to get a fine tilth.
At the time of last ploughing, apply FYM (5t/ac) and biofertilizers viz., 1.0 kg each of Azospirillum, Phosphobacteria (or) Azophos, and Silicate solubilizing bacteria (SSB) mixed with 10kg of FYM and 10kg of sand, per acre.
Seed Treatment
As in irrigated cotton.
Treat the seeds with Imidacloprid 70 WS @ 10 g/kg seed before sowing for sucking pest management
Spacing
Adopt spacing of 100 x 10 cm or 90 x 15 cm
Sowing
Dibble the seeds at a depth of 3-5 cm using Pneumatic seed drill.
Fertilizer Application
Follow the fertilizer schedule as below
Irrigated :  40:20:20 Kg N: P2O5: K2O /ac.
Rainfed: 20:10:20 Kg N: P2O5: K2O /ac
Apply 50 % of N and K2O, full dose of P2O5 as basal and remaining 50 % of N and K2O @  40 – 45 DAS.
Weed Management
Spray Pendimethalin (38.7% CS) @ 260 ml/ acre or pendimethalin (30% EC) 400 ml/acre within third day after sowing followed by one power weeding or hand weeding on 45 DAS.
Spray pyrithiobac sodium @ 25 g/ac + quizalofop ethyl @20 g/ac at 2 to 4 leaf stage of the weeds, If post emergence application of herbicide is warranted.
Physiological Intervention
Spray Cotton Plus @ 2.5 kg/acre mixed in 200 litres of water or Spray 40 ppm of NAA (40 mg of NAA dissolved in one litre of water) at flowering and at boll formation stages
Water Management
Irrigate immediately after sowing and followed by life irrigation on 5th day after sowing
Regulate subsequent irrigations according to the soil type, prevailing weather conditions and receipt of rains.
Ensure irrigation at critical crop growth stage – Square formation and boll maturity stage.
Canopy Management
Spray Mepiquat Chloride (0.015%) at flowering and boll development stage
Plant Protection
Pest and disease management to be done by employing self propelled high clearance boom sprayer
Defoliator Spray
Apply defoliants like sodium chlorate (0.9%) at 60% boll bursting stage.
Harvesting
Single harvest @ 140 DAS
